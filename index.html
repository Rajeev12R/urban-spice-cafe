Our Story

Brand Story

Born from a
Passion for Craft

Urban Spice Café was born in 2018 when founder Arjit Batra returned from a year of travelling through coffee farms in Colombia, Ethiopia, and Vietnam with one obsession: bringing the world's finest coffee culture to his hometown of Amritsar.

What started as a modest 20-seat café on Lawrence Road has grown into Punjab's most celebrated coffee destination — a place where the aroma of freshly roasted beans mingles with the warmth of Punjabi hospitality.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To create a community space where exceptional coffee, honest food, and genuine human connection come together. We exist to make every guest feel profoundly welcomed.

Our Vision

To be the most beloved café brand in North India, recognised for quality that never compromises, sustainability that inspires, and an experience that transcends the ordinary.
